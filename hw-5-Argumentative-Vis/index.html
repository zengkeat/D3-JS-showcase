United State and China - The two most CO2 emitter in the world:

Who should take more responsibility to reduce the CO2 emission?

Dataset: https://www.iea.org/data-and-statistics/data-product/greenhouse-gas-emissions-from-energy-highlights

Introduction:
China and US are the highest producers of CO2 in the world, in total they accounted for more than 38% of global CO2 emision in 2019. China emision alone is more than half of that figure. Thus, many people bashing China for poluting the world and demand China to take more responsibility on reducing carbon emision. But the situation is far from straightforward - measuring a country total carbon emission without considered its population doesn't always paint the most accurate image. Therefore, looking at the total CO2 emission of both country VS their per-capita emission can add nuance to the overall debate of which country should take more responsibility to reduce the CO2 emission.
